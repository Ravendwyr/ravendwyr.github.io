Among the new changes made to World of Warcraft in Patch 6.1 was a new jukebox that can change the background music while in your Garrison.  Seven of the current total of 31 music scrolls are given to you for free on completion of the jukebox, and the rest must be bought or looted from all over Azeroth.

There are also three achievements for collecting the music rolls: [Ten Hit Tunes](https://www.wowhead.com/achievement=9828/ten-hit-tunes) for collecting ten, [Azeroth's Top Twenty Tunes](https://www.wowhead.com/achievement=9912/azeroths-top-twenty-tunes") for collecting twenty, and [Full Discography](https://www.wowhead.com/achievement=10015/full-discography) for collecting them all.  The achievement for collecting twenty rolls also awards a [portable jukebox toy](https://www.wowhead.com/item=122700/portable-audiophone).

This new addon is designed to help music collectors everywhere by adding marker pins to the worldmap and minimap in a similar style to my existing HandyNotes plugins for the [Lunar Festival](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes_lunarfestival), [Summer Festival](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes_summerfestival). and [Hallow's End](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes_hallowsend) Hallow's End in-game events.

These marker pins show the locations of any music rolls you have not yet added to your jukebox.  Each pin also has a brief explanation on how to obtain its corresponding music roll and will automatically hide itself once you have added the roll to your jukebox.

Unlike my existing HandyNotes plugins, these pins are shown all year round and do not need any in-game events to be active in order to function.  Simply install the addon and go hunting!

HandyNotes Azeroth's Top Tunes is available for download from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes_azerothstoptunes) and requires [HandyNotes](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes) to work.
