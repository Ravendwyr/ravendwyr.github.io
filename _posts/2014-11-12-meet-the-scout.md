Warlords of Draenor goes live on EU servers in thirty minutes!  Whoo!

This time, I'm going to do things a little differently.  Every expansion launch up until now I have been mostly playing as Alliance.

This time, I'm going in as a member of the Red Team.

[Scout Arnvaldt](https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/eu/draenor/arnvaldt) was the first character I created when I began playing World of Warcraft in May 2005.  I had neglected him for the last couple of years, only popping on now and again for world events and the like.  I managed to get him to Level 60 before the Burning Crusade expansion launched, and pretty much stayed there for a long time.  I eventually managed to get him up to Level 73 while questing with my fiancée's Priest, and I used the free character boost that came with pre-ordering the Warlords of Draenor expansion to finally get him to Level 90.

Arnvaldt will be my main throughout the Draenor campaign, but I will do my best to make sure my other characters get some love as well.

Lok'tar ogar, heroes. Victory or death!
