Hallow's End will be upon us in the next few days, and the HandyNotes plugin for the world event has been updated in preparation for its arrival.

Similar to recent updates to the Lunar Festival and Summer Festival plugins, HandyNotes Hallow's End now features an automatic on/off switch and a one-click waypoint feature.  Please note that the plugin now requires the core HandyNotes addon at Version 1.4.0 or newer to work correctly.  Attempting to use an older version of HandyNotes will create a message in the chatbox warning you that the plugin will not work until you update.

This release is a small maintenance release to bring the plugin up to date for Patch 7.0.3 and the Legion expansion pack.  More updates will be made in the near future for when Patch 7.1 launches on October 25th this year.

The new version of HandyNotes Hallow's End is available to download from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes_hallowsend) and requires [HandyNotes](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes) to work.

Hallow's End 2016 begins on October 18th and runs until November 1st.  As always, if you find any problems or error messages please let me know and I'll do my best to solve the problem.

Good luck!
