Today's update to HandyNotes Lunar Festival is a fairly minor one which adds some quality-of-life changes to make finding the Elders even easier than before.

As of Version 1.1.5, the addon now displays the locations of Elders inside dungeon instances on the zone and continent maps.  The marker pins are placed either at the dungeon entrance itself (for example, Zul'Farrak and Gundrak) or at the entrance of the cave where the dungeon entrance is located inside (for example, Sunken Temple and Maraudon).

The new version of HandyNotes Lunar Festival is available to download from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes_lunarfestival) and requires [HandyNotes](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes) to work.
