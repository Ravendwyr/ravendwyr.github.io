Version 1.1.2 of HandyNotes Lunar Festival was released earlier today.  This updates solves a few issues with "phased" zones, such as Uldum and Mount Hyjal.

Patch Notes:

* Fixed map marker pins not always showing up in "phased" zones.
* Fixed a bug that prevented waypoints from being created in "phased" zones.
* Added the name of the Elder NPC to the context menu.

You can download the new version of HandyNotes Lunar Festival from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/handynotes_lunarfestival).

The Lunar Festival will end on February 9th 2013 so unless any other problems appear, development of this addon will begin to slow down.
