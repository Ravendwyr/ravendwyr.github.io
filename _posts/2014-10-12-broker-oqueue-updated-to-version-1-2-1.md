Broker oQueue is back!

In a rather surprising turn of events, oQueue has been resurrected and is now working with Patch 6.0.2.  As such, Broker oQueue has also been resurrected.

Today's update to Broker oQueue is only a small maintenance update to mark it as up-to-date with the Warlords of Draenor pre-patch.  No new features have been added this time and all existing functionality remains the same as the previous version.

The new update is available for download from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/broker_oqueue) and is now also available on [Solid ICE](https://solidice.com/downloads/world-of-warcraft/brokeroqueue)!  If you have any questions or feedback then please do not hesitate to get in touch.

Enjoy!
