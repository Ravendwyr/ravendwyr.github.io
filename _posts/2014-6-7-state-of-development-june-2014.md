The observant among you will have noticed that I haven't been very active lately.

The truth is, I needed to take a break from WoW for a while. I had a disagreement with a high-ranking guild member after a failed Heroic Firelands fun run and haven't logged in since. My disappearance has nothing to do with a certain sexist article from a certain Blizzard employee, although it definitely didn't help.

I also moved house in February and we're still unpacking and decorating. But the computers are set up and we have carpets! And furniture! And stuff!

So what does this mean for the addons I maintain? I haven't given them the regular maintenance I usually do but I haven't forgotten about them either. I have received several feature requests for MogIt Highlighter and I would love to integrate them. And with the Warlords of Draenor expansion due for release in December, the majority of my addons will need a patch to bring them back up to date.

I will return to WoW one day. I miss playing and I have some exciting updates I want to make to my addons (well, they're exciting for me at least). For example, TradeSkillInfo now has a working dataminer so maintaining the recipe database will be a hell of a lot easier!

This is not "goodbye". It's more of a "be back in a bit".
