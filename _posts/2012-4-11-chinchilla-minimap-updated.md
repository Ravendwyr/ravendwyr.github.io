Chinchilla Minimap has been updated to Version 2.5.3.

This is mostly just a maintenance release. The only notable change is that the embedded libraries Chinchilla Minimap uses have been updated in an effort to prevent UI taint. Other than that, the functionality is virtually unchanged from Version 2.5.2.

You can download the updated version from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/chinchilla) or from [WoWInterface](https://www.wowinterface.com/downloads/info8394-ChinchillaMinimap.html).
