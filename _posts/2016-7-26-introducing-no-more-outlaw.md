In the Legion pre-patch, the Combat specialisation for rogues was renamed to Outlaw as part of the talent overhaul.  While some people welcomed this change, others did not like it and were not entirely happy about being considered outlaws.

Introducing "No More Outlaw".  This purely cosmetic and incredibly simple addon will replace all instances of the word "Outlaw" with "Combat" in Blizzard's talent interface and character frame.  It even works when inspecting other Rogues!

The addon does not change the description or the icon of the specialisation; it will only change the name.  However, if enough people wish for this to happen I can investigate further.  I should also point out the addon only works for people who install it; people who do not install the addon will still see you as an Outlaw when they inspect your character.

This new addon can be downloaded right now from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/no-more-outlaw).  If you find any problems or if you have any questions, feel free to get in touch and I will help as best as I can.
