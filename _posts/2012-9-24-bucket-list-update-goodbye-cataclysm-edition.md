There's less than three hours left until Mists of Pandaria launches on EU realms and it's high time I posted an update for my bucket list.

* **Transfer Characters** - Done. Ulbrecht and Innis have been successfully transferred and are enjoying their new home!
* **Buy Heirlooms** - Done. I have gathered all the agility leather heirlooms, a weapon, trinkets and even managed to snag the [Dread Pirate Ring](https://www.wowhead.com/item=50255/dread-pirate-ring) for my Pandaren Monk!
* **Loremaster** - Failed. I ran out of time. Patch 5.0.4 came out shortly after I posted about my preparation plans and added [Loremaster of Pandaria](https://www.wowhead.com/achievement=6541/loremaster-of-pandaria) as a requirement to the meta achievement. [Icecrown](https://www.wowhead.com/achievement=40/icecrown-the-final-goal) was the last zone I needed to complete, but I simply ran out of time.
* **Max out Ysaera's Professions** - Failed. I am happy with the progress I made and managed to max out Ysaera's cooking, fishing, and first aid successfully. I neglected her archaeology and it remains at 165/225.
* **Transmogrification** - Done. All of Ysaera's planned transmogrification items have been obtained, along with a few bonus items that fit the look very well.
* **Level Alts to 85** - Failed. Suranyu remains at Level 84. I tried my best but I couldn't find the time or the willingness to continue levelling.
* **Collect Mounts and Minipets** - Thanks to account-wide minipets I now have 168 unique companions in my collection, enough to get the [celestial dragon](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=40624/celestial-dragon) companion.
* **Get as Much Gold as Possible** - I managed to raise a total of over 120,000 gold across all of my characters. Not bad.

Despite not completing everything I wanted to do, overall I am happy with the progress I made. There's plenty left for me to get on with in Pandaria, and lots of other things in the "old worlds" to complete if I get the urge to.
